About Us

Komala Scented Supari is a 51-year-old heritage shop started by HK Ramaswamy, located opposite to Basaveshwara Temple, Gandhi Bazaar, Shivamogga. What began as a humble local venture has grown into a trusted brand cherished by generations.

Our journey is rooted in tradition, quality, and customer trust. Each batch of supari is crafted with care — whether it's our smooth whole nut selections or our flavorful powdered blends. Among our offerings, the Masala Supari stands as our signature blend, known for its unique mix of elaichi and sweet diamond sugar.

We take pride in serving not just a product, but a piece of legacy — one that connects people through taste, tradition, and timeless quality.
